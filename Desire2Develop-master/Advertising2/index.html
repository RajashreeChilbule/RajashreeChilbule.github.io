Advertising

FM

FM advertising is a fantastic medium for local advertisers as it allows them to reach out to the local community in a highly targeted and cost effective way.

Social Media

Social media advertising is a term used to describe online advertising that focus on social networking sites like Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, etc.

Event Marketing

Event marketing is the experiential marketing of a brand, service, or product through promotional events. It typically involves direct interaction with a brand's representatives.

Hording

Advertising hordings are a common print promotional technique used by big businesses. A hording includes a visual design, images, colors and copy.

Staff Skill Management

Advertising Managers direct a company's advertising activities and staff members as develop creative, consistent brand-specific ad campaigns.
